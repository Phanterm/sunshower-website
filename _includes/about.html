About

Sunshower is a boutique cooperative game studio created to fulfill the need for high quality, lovingly hand-crafted experiences. We're made up of industry vets who know how to analyze and understand current and historical trends, and we pride ourselves on these abilities to chart our course.

Our mission is simple: deliver iconic games that will withstand the test of time.

Part of achieving and sustaining that mission statement is our philosophy -- to weather the storm in a demanding industry that changes every day.

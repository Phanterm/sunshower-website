		About

		<div class="about-content">
			<p class="smallcaps">Sunshower is a small independent game cooperative formed in service to high quality, lovingly hand-crafted experiences.</p>
			
			<p>Our mission is simple: deliver iconic games that will withstand the test of time. The way to achieve and sustain that mission statement is through our philosophy of weathering the storm in a demanding industry that changes every day.</p>

			<p>But great games need great people to bring them to life.  There are no bosses; each of us is a leader. There are no discrepancies in salary; each of us has equal representation. At Sunshower, each of us owns our work.</p>
			
			<p>Our name is fitting for many reasons besides the local weather in Seattle. Sunlit rain is a rare and beautiful thing, providing simultaneously quiet contemplation and bright warmth. And whether you prefer your days sunny or rainy, sunshowers have something for everyone. As both artists and artisans, the spirit we bring to our games is a celebration of expression and work well done.</p>

		About

		<div class="article-blank-content">
			<p>Sunshower is a small indie game cooperative created to fulfill the need for high quality, lovingly hand-crafted experiences. Our mission is simple: deliver iconic games that will withstand the test of time. The way to achieve and sustain that mission statement is through our philosophy of weathering the storm in a demanding industry that changes every day.</p>
		</div>
		<div class="article-filled-content">
			<p>Our studio was founded not simply to make great games, but to allow the great people who bring those games to life to own their work. The name "Sunshower" is fitting for many reasons besides the local weather. Sunshowers are a rare and beautiful thing, providing simultaneously quiet contemplation and bright warmth. Whether you prefer your days sunny or rainy, sunshowers have something for everyone. As both artists and artisans, the spirit we bring to our games is a celebration of expression and work well done.</p>
